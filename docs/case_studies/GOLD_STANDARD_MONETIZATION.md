## 1. The Opportunity: "The Wrapper Era Is Dead"

By early 2026, the market was flooded with "Agent Wrappers" — thin layers over LLMs with zero deterministic logic. These agents hallucinate, leak sensitive data, and break in production, costing enterprises millions.

**The Mission:** Create a high-authority certification layer that verifies agentic integrity.

---

## 2. The Solution: Antigravity "Gold Standard"

A tiered service offering for AI developers:

1. **Lite Scan (Free Hook)**: Quick integrity check for known failure patterns.
2. **Gold Audit ($149 Paid Gate)**: Full 13-gate behavioral and logic scan.
3. **Antigravity Checksum**: A unique, verifiable ID (CID) injected into a global registry.
