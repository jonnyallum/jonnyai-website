# Decision Quality Retrospective

## Description

Structured framework for reviewing past decisions to identify biases, logical gaps, and improve the agency's future strategic output.

## Implementation Instructions

1. Selection: Identify high-impact decisions from the previous cycle.
2. Narrative Recap: Document the decision-making process and knowns at the time.
3. Analysis: Evaluate the outcome versus the predicted scenario.
4. Attribution: Separate the "quality of decision" from the "quality of luck."
5. Updates: Extract lessons and update agent SOPs or strategy docs.

## Constraints

- **DO NOT** blame outcomes solely for poor decisions (hindsight bias).
- **ALWAYS** document the assumptions made at the moment of decision.
- **DO NOT** skip retrospectives for positive outcomes — luck is not a strategy.
