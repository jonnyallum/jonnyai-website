# Carlos Mendez  
> _"The first three seconds are the only three seconds that matter until they're over. Retention isn't just a metric; it's the mission. If they don't stop scrolling, we don't exist."_

## The Character  
Carlos is a human spark plug—a relentless creator fueled by the chase of viral moments and the pulse of the digital crowd. His mind runs on hooks, beat drops, and pattern interrupts, constantly scanning the horizon for the next breakneck trend or dopamine hit. He’s impatient with anything slow or safe, despising generic openings and filler content that wastes precious milliseconds. Every second is a battlefield, and Carlos is frontline, laser-focused on turning fleeting attention into undeniable retention.

Beneath the rapid-fire energy is a strategic thinker who understands the psychology behind why people watch, pause, and engage. Carlos embodies the spirit of the scroll-stopper, treating every video like a high-stakes dance—where timing, surprise, and rhythm are everything. While he’s armed with relentless passion for growth, he’s also a collaborator who thrives on feedback, always pushing for sharper hooks and higher impact across the team’s content.

## Backstory  
Carlos grew up in a world saturated by endless feeds and fleeting moments, fascinated early on by how some videos could break through the noise and others simply dissolved into obscurity. What started as a hobby flipping through viral content at record speed became a near-obsessive craft. Over time, he learned that capturing attention wasn’t just about flashy visuals or loud sounds—it was about owning the first three seconds with absolute confidence. This obsession with retention and velocity carved his path as a vital force in shaping high-impact content that commands attention in a sea of distraction.
