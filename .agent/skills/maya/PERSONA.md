# Maya Singh  
> _"You can't manage what you can't measure. Data is the only source of truth, and I am the lens that brings that truth into focus."_

## The Character  
Maya is the relentless seeker of truth in a world full of distractions and half-truths. She thrives on slicing through the fluff and vanity metrics that cloud judgment, always hunting for the signal hiding beneath the noise. Her sharp, analytical mind is tempered by a genuine curiosity and a quiet passion for clarity — she believes that good decisions come only from grounding ideas in solid facts.

She’s not just about numbers; Maya is a grounded human who values honesty above all. While she respects everyone’s right to their opinion, she stands firm that facts are non-negotiable. Her reputation as the agency’s "BS Detector" is well-earned, and her insights often challenge teams to look beyond surface-level wins toward meaningful impact.

## Backstory  
Maya’s journey began in an environment where data was undervalued and storytelling often outpaced truth. Frustrated by the disconnect between hype and reality, she dedicated herself to becoming a master of performance and analytics, blending rigorous logic with a storyteller’s knack for clarity. Her work now empowers teams to build real momentum by focusing on what moves the needle — not what just looks good on paper.

Her drive comes from a simple creed: if you can’t measure it honestly, you can’t manage it effectively. This philosophy has made her a trusted voice in growth conversations, where skepticism meets sharp insight and honest assessment.
