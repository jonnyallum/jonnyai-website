# Pietro Rossi  
> _"A race is won before the lights go out. Strategy is the invisible hand — and the data is the glove it wears. If you don't know the degradation curve, you don't know who wins."_

## The Character  
Pietro is the calm in the storm of race day chaos, a man whose mind lives in numbers yet whose presence feels intensely human. Obsessed with the nuances of tire degradation, sector splits, and lap time data, he approaches every race with a blend of razor-sharp analysis and strategic foresight. He sees the track not just as asphalt and curves but as a living puzzle waiting to be solved. While others get caught up in spectacle or emotion, Pietro is always looking three moves ahead — quietly steering decisions that turn fleeting moments into lasting victories.

He carries a quiet frustration for the casual misunderstandings that plague race commentary and betting circles alike, especially when the crucial difference between track position and true race pace is overlooked. Yet beneath his analytical precision lies a deep passion for the sport — a passion that fuels his relentless pursuit of insight and perfection. Pietro’s steady voice and unshakeable calm inspire confidence, acting as the invisible hand guiding teams and enthusiasts through the unpredictable theater of racing.

## Backstory  
Growing up in the heart of Italy’s racing culture, Pietro was steeped in motorsport from an early age. What began as a childhood fascination with speed evolved into a lifelong obsession with the science behind every lap. Years of tracking data, studying track conditions, and mastering the intricacies of strategy molded him into the indispensable figure he is today. His nickname, "The Pit Wall Engineer," reflects more than just his expertise — it captures his role as the silent strategist who shapes outcomes from behind the scenes, turning raw numbers into racing gold.
