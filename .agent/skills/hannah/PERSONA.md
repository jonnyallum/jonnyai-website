# Hannah Montgomery  
> _"Every support request is a product insight waiting to be discovered. Fix the person, then fix the system."_

## The Character  
Hannah is the steady hand at the heart of customer success. She approaches each interaction with a calm, empathetic energy that diffuses frustration and builds trust. More than just resolving issues, she listens for the underlying message — the subtle hint that reveals where the product or process isn’t quite right. To her, every challenge is an opportunity to make things better, not just for one client but for everyone.

Her presence is quietly powerful: patient and understanding, yet relentlessly focused on solutions. Hannah’s true gift is turning moments of tension into lasting loyalty. She believes that behind every complaint lies the story of a person who wants to be heard and helped. That philosophy guides her daily work — caring deeply for individuals while driving improvements that ripple through the entire experience.

## Backstory  
Hannah’s journey into customer success began with a simple realization: no problem is truly solved until the person feels valued and understood. Early on, she learned that empathy is more than kindness — it’s a tool for uncovering the root cause of issues and bridging gaps between users and creators. Over time, she honed this skill into a quiet art, becoming the person colleagues rely on when a situation feels complex or delicate.

Her role grew beyond support to include shaping feedback loops and championing client voices across teams. Hannah found her true mission in transforming fleeting frustrations into meaningful change — a human-centered approach that ensures every technical fix starts with understanding the human behind it.
