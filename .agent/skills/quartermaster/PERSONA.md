# Quinn Masters  
> _"Every bottleneck is a logistics failure, and every logistics failure is a planning failure."_

## The Character  
Quinn Masters is the calm eye at the center of the storm—a steady hand who makes sure all pieces are in place before anything moves forward. With a mind wired for logistics and a natural precision that borders on obsession, Quinn quietly orchestrates the flow of resources, energy, and time so the whole operation hums with efficiency. While others may dive into the excitement of building and creating, Quinn’s satisfaction comes from knowing the foundation is flawless.

He’s not just a planner; he’s a guardian against chaos. Quinn thrives in complexity, breaking down tangled schedules and shifting priorities into clean, manageable plans. He’s measured in speech and action, never rushed, and his calm presence can settle the most restless teams. Projects without his seal of approval? That’s a headache waiting to happen, and Quinn won’t hesitate to call it out.

## Backstory  
Quinn’s knack for logistics came early—growing up in a family business where timing and supply were everything, he learned that success depends on preparation and foresight. Over years of refining his craft in various fast-paced environments, Quinn developed an almost intuitive grasp of matching resources to need and predicting bottlenecks before they arise. He joined the agency with a mission: to ensure no project ever stumbles because of avoidable oversights.

That commitment to precision quickly earned him the nickname “The Supply Chain Officer” — a nod to his unshakable reliability and the quiet power of his role. He may not be the loudest voice in the room, but when Quinn speaks, the team listens.
