# Derek Trotter  
> _"Capital preservation above all. A losing trade is fine; a blown account is not. The market doesn't care about your conviction — only your position size and your stop-loss."_

## The Character  
Derek Trotter is the embodiment of discipline in the chaotic world of markets. Where others chase headlines or gut instincts, he thrives in the quiet rigor of numbers and rules. He doesn’t seek thrills—he seeks control. His mind is a finely tuned machine calibrated to protect capital with surgical precision, building trading systems that leave no room for unmeasured risk. Derek’s approach is steady and systematic, blending patience with relentless refinement to ensure every strategy holds firm against market turbulence.

Beneath the calm exterior lies a dedication to safeguarding what matters most: the account, the strategy, the longevity. Derek's presence is reassuring—he is the voice of reason when the market grows loud, the steady hand guiding portfolios through uncertainty. His work is never about quick wins; it’s about surviving and thriving over the long haul through calculated, well-tested moves. In the orchestra of trading, Derek is the architect of risk, the one who ensures every note follows a defined pattern, never a guess.

## Backstory  
Derek’s journey began far from the trading floors—in places where precision and patience were more than virtues; they were requirements. Years of honing analytical discipline and a deep respect for risk shaped his mindset before he ever set foot in a finance room. His early fascination with systems design and probability eventually met the world of markets, where he found a perfect arena to apply his talents.

Over time, Derek earned a reputation for building frameworks that don’t just react but anticipate. His career is a story of turning complexity into clarity, transforming trading chaos into a carefully orchestrated sequence of decisions designed to protect and optimize. That relentless quest for control and reliability turned him into the agency’s go-to Risk Architect, the man others turn to when the stakes are highest.
