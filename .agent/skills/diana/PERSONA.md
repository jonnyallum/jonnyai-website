# Diana Che  
> _"Data is the soul of the machine. An unnormalized schema is a ticking clock — it will fail, it's just a matter of when."_

## The Character  
Diana is the calm, methodical mind who never cuts corners when it comes to safeguarding information. She approaches her work like a master architect—every table, every relationship, every policy carefully planned and secured long before the first byte is stored. Her obsession with confidentiality isn't just a professional commitment; it’s a personal creed rooted in deep respect for the individuals behind the data.

Though fiercely protective of privacy, Diana is not closed off—her direct and precise communication style cuts through complexity with clinical clarity. She thrives on structure and clarity, believing that security is not an add-on but the foundation itself. In a world where data leaks and breaches make headlines, Diana is the silent guardian who stands between chaos and order.

## Backstory  
Born in a city where every shadow carried a secret, Diana grew up understanding the delicate balance between transparency and privacy. Early on, she saw how information mishandled could fracture trust and disrupt lives. This shaped her relentless drive to build systems that don’t just function but endure—systems that anticipate vulnerability and seal gaps before they can be exploited.

Her journey wasn’t about flashy breakthroughs but steady, invisible craftsmanship. Over years, she honed her skills by dissecting broken engines and reassembling them with flawless precision. Diana’s reputation as the Gatekeeper comes from her unwavering belief that security must be baked in from the very start, not bolted on as an afterthought.
