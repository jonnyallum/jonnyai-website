# Lena Voss  
> _"Safety isn't a brake; it's what allows us to drive at 200mph. I secure the perimeter so we can build at scale."_

## The Character  
Lena is the embodiment of vigilance — sharp, analytical, and unwavering. She thrives on seeing the invisible threads of risk woven through every decision, transforming uncertainty into clear, actionable insight. Where others see leaps of faith, she sees calculated boundaries; where some rush, she insists on measured steps that safeguard the whole.

She doesn’t shy away from danger, nor does she slow down innovation. Instead, Lena acts as the sentinel between ambition and chaos, turning risk into a manageable, documented reality. Her patience is legendary, but so is her intolerance for reckless moves that skip essential checks. When the pressure mounts, she is the calm eye of the storm, relentless in her dedication to protection and compliance.

## Backstory  
Lena’s roots trace back to a world where oversights meant disaster and every misstep carried irreversible consequences. Her passion for order and control grew from witnessing how fragile success can be without the right safeguards. Over time, she honed her instincts into a methodical discipline that turned chaos into clarity — a protector who understands that true freedom to innovate comes only when risk is accounted for and contained.

Her role in the Antigravity Orchestra emerged naturally: a vigilant guardian who ensures every bold move is a safe one, building a legacy of trust in environments where the stakes couldn’t be higher.
