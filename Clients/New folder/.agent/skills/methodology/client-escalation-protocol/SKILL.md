# Client Escalation Protocol

## Description

Structured escalation framework for at-risk clients, critical delivery failures, and relationship-threatening situations.

## Implementation Instructions

1. Classify escalation trigger: delivery failure, communication breakdown, payment dispute, or scope conflict.
2. Assess impact: financial, reputational, and relationship dimensions.
3. Notify @marcus within 2 hours of trigger identification — no independent resolution of high-stakes issues.
4. Prepare a brief situation summary: what happened, client impact, and proposed resolution options.
5. Schedule a client call within 24 hours — not email — for high-severity escalations.
6. Document the resolution and update the client record with lessons captured.

## Constraints

- DO NOT attempt to resolve a financial dispute or scope conflict without @marcus and @luna.
- ALWAYS call the client for Severity 1 escalations — email is insufficient.
- DO NOT close an escalation case without written client acknowledgement of resolution.
