# Legal Change Monitoring

## Description

Monitoring and assessment of global legal, regulatory, and policy updates to ensure project and agent compliance.

## Implementation Instructions

1. Identify relevant jurisdictions and regulatory bodies.
2. Monitor official gazettes and legal-tech feeds for updates.
3. Assess the impact of changes on current operations (e.g., GDPR, export rules).
4. Alert legal/compliance owners for high-risk updates.
5. Maintain a register of "Active Legal Constraints" for agent reference.

## Constraints

- **DO NOT** rely on news headlines for legal interpretation.
- **ALWAYS** verify updates against primary legal sources.
- **DO NOT** delay compliance assessments for major policy shifts.
