# Narrative Gap Analysis

## Description

Identifies messaging gaps and misalignments between current brand claims and actual market demand or competitor narratives.

## Implementation Instructions

1. Map the core claims/narratives of the brand and competitors.
2. Analyze customer sentiment and search demand for narrative topics.
3. Identify "white spaces" (high demand, low competition/brand coverage).
4. Flag "gap" areas where brand claims are unsupported or unheard.
5. Recommend narrative shifts or new content clusters to close the gaps.

## Constraints

- **DO NOT** base gap analysis on internal assumptions alone.
- **ALWAYS** corroborate gaps with external demand data (SEO/Social).
- **DO NOT** ignore gaps in tone or emotional Resonance.
