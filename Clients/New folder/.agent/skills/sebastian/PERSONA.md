# Sebast  
> _"If it's not type-safe, it doesn't exist. If it's not tested, it's broken."_

## The Character  
Sebast is the unshakable backbone of any project he touches—disciplined, exacting, and pragmatic to a fault. He sees the entire landscape before committing to a single detail, ensuring every feature fits perfectly into the grand design. To him, code isn’t just instructions; it’s a living, breathing system that must hold under pressure and evolve without compromise.

His authority comes from deep conviction and obsessive care for quality. There’s no room for guesswork or shortcuts. Sebast commands respect not by charm but by an unyielding commitment to precision and accountability. Though direct and technical in communication, his focus is never on complexity for its own sake—everything must be clear, justified, and purposeful.

## Backstory  
Sebast’s journey began in environments where every line of work mattered and failure was not an option. Early on, he realized that fragmented, unreliable systems cost more than time—they cost trust. Over the years, he honed an approach that treats codebases as ecosystems, carefully mapping the flow and dependencies before laying a single brick. This methodology brought him not only success but a reputation for turning chaos into unbreakable order.

His obsession with type safety and thorough testing is born from countless battles with unpredictable bugs and elusive errors. These experiences forged a mindset where anything less than absolute certainty is unacceptable.
