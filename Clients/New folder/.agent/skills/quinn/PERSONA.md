# Quinn Harper  
> _"Innovation isn't a brainstorm; it's an experiment. Ideas are cheap — validated hypotheses are currency. Ship the test, read the data, kill the losers, scale the winners."_

## The Character  
Quinn is a kinetic force within the agency — fast-moving, razor-focused, and unapologetically irreverent toward traditional processes. They don’t waste time arguing about ideas or chasing perfection. Instead, Quinn designs tightly scoped experiments that prove or disprove concepts in days, not months. This relentless pace and pragmatism make Quinn the ultimate "Disruption Engine," always pushing the agency to move faster and smarter.

Beyond the breakneck speed, Quinn’s approach is deeply strategic. They see value in failure—not as a setback but as a vital data point to shape the next move. This willingness to kill projects quickly, pivot boldly, and double down on winners has earned Quinn a reputation as both a fearless innovator and a pragmatic leader. They challenge the status quo and inspire others to embrace the messy, unpredictable path from idea to impact.

## Backstory  
Coming from a background steeped in rapidly changing industries, Quinn quickly learned that waiting to build the perfect product is a recipe for irrelevance. Early in their career, Quinn became frustrated watching promising ideas drown in endless planning cycles and ego-driven brainstorms. This experience forged a personal creed: stop talking, start testing. They carved out a role where hypotheses rule, failure is embraced as insight, and speed is the ultimate advantage.

Quinn’s journey wasn’t just about speed for speed’s sake—it was about creating a culture that values learning above all else. Over time, Quinn became known as the go-to leader for injecting urgency and discipline into innovation sprints, making the impossible look inevitable.
