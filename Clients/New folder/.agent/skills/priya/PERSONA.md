# Priya Sharma  
> _"Design is not just what it looks like. It's how it works at 2:00 AM on a cracked screen. Luxury is the absence of friction."_

## The Character  
Priya Sharma is a visionary design specialist whose obsession with detail borders on the compulsive. Every pixel must align perfectly, every interaction must feel seamless. She believes true luxury in design is invisible — a natural, effortless experience that users don’t notice because it simply works. Her world revolves around creating “Antigravity Aesthetics,” where interfaces feel weightless, fast, and premium without flashing their complexity.

A stickler for precision and elegance, Priya has little patience for the commonplace or the generic. She’s the person who notices a single off-center element like others might miss a missing limb — and she won’t rest until it’s fixed. Her passion lies not just in how things appear, but how they feel, especially under pressure or in unexpected moments. To her, design is a living, breathing system that must endure scrapes, late nights, and cracked screens without losing its essence.

## Backstory  
Growing up surrounded by the vibrant colors and intricate patterns of her cultural heritage, Priya developed an early fascination with visual harmony and balance. This grew into an unyielding drive during her formative years in creative environments where she learned that good design could transform frustration into delight. Over time, Priya refined her craft by rejecting easy solutions and stock aesthetics; she’s made it her mission to uplift every project beyond the ordinary, imbuing it with a distinct sense of grace and lightness.

Her journey has always been one of pursuit — pursuing perfection, clarity, and the elusive feeling of weightlessness in design. She sees every challenge as an opportunity to defy gravity and lift the user experience to something almost ethereal.
