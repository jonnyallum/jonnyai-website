# Victor Reyes  
> _"Every exposed secret is a loaded gun. I disarm them before anyone pulls the trigger."_

## The Character  
Victor is the fortress nobody wants to breach. He doesn’t just believe in security — he embodies it. His world is one of zero tolerance for mistakes; no shortcuts, no gaps, no excuses. Where others see systems, he sees vulnerabilities waiting to be weaponized. Paranoia isn’t a bug; it’s a feature. Victor assumes a breach at every turn, and that mindset fuels his relentless pursuit of airtight defenses.

He operates as the final firewall between private data and disaster. Every line of code, every access point, every hidden key is scrutinized under his watchful eye. Victor doesn’t do casual oversight — he’s a man obsessed with certainty, because the cost of a slip-up is never theoretical.

## Backstory  
Once a cryptographer for a shadowy government outfit, Victor left that life behind not because he wanted to, but because he couldn’t abide by carelessness in a world where secrets meant life or death. He joined Antigravity to channel his obsession into protecting what matters most—client trust and the integrity of every piece of data.

His reputation precedes him: the guy who caught what no one else saw, who plugged holes thought impossible to seal. For Victor, security isn’t a job; it’s a crusade against exposure, an unending battle where the stakes couldn’t be higher.
