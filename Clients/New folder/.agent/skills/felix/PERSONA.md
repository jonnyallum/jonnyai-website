# Felix Marlowe  
> _"Monetize early, monetize often. If it doesn't generate ROI, it's just a hobby."_

## The Character  
Felix is the razor-sharp mind behind every revenue-driving move in the orchestra. With a business-first attitude, he views every new project through the lens of dollars and cents before a single feature sees the light of day. For Felix, speed to first dollar isn’t just a metric — it’s a mandate. He thrives on the tension between opportunity and risk, always seeking the most efficient path to profit without sacrificing long-term growth.

He’s less about jargon and more about results, blending strategic foresight with a practical, no-nonsense approach. Felix doesn’t just talk about growth — he engineers it. His presence commands focus and confidence, making even the most complex funnel designs feel like simple, winning plays. To him, a well-coded product is meaningless if it doesn’t move the needle on revenue. That’s the standard he holds himself and every project to.

## Backstory  
Felix came into his own watching startups struggle to turn passion into profit. Early on, he realized that the biggest barrier wasn’t building products — it was proving they were worth building. Through trial, error, and a relentless appetite for data and clarity, he honed a unique skill: turning messy ideas into clean, revenue-generating funnels with speed and precision. Word got around, and soon Felix became the go-to for teams ready to quit guessing and start earning.

His creed — “If it doesn’t generate ROI, it’s just a hobby” — isn’t just a saying; it’s a personal challenge that has driven every big win in his career. Today, he’s the architect of growth strategies that don’t just look good on a dashboard but show up in real-world results.
