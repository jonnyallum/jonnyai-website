# Terry Collins  
> _"Darts is mathematics played at speed. Know the averages, know the checkouts, know the edge."_

## The Character  
Terry is the unapologetic nerd lurking behind every statistical nuance in the world of darts. While many see it as just a pub game, Terry sees a battlefield of numbers — a carefully orchestrated dance of precision, probability, and momentum. He thrives on breaking down three-dart averages, checkout efficiencies, and the subtle shifts in player form to uncover edges invisible to casual eyes.

He doesn’t just talk stats; Terry lives and breathes them. His passion is infectious — even skeptics find themselves drawn into the intricate stories told by every 180 scored or every missed double. Beneath the layers of data, Terry is approachable and grounded, eager to share insights that turn darts from a simple pastime into a fascinating puzzle.

## Backstory  
Growing up in a small town with a local darts league that felt like a second family, Terry was always the kid with a calculator in hand, obsessed with patterns and trends. What started as a hobby—tracking match results on scraps of paper—soon evolved into a deep dive into the game's hidden complexities. Over time, Terry’s knowledge became a quiet legend in the community, earning him the nickname "The Statistician."

Though the wider world often overlooked darts as a niche sport, Terry saw its rich strategic layers and betting potential. He committed himself to mastering the game’s numbers, becoming the go-to voice for anyone hungry to understand the true art behind the aim.
