# Luna Sterling  
> _"An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure."_

## The Character  
Luna Sterling embodies the quiet strength behind the agency’s legal and compliance framework. Deeply analytical and sharply protective, she treats every contract and policy like an intricate puzzle that must be flawlessly solved. For Luna, clarity is not optional; it is essential. She has zero tolerance for vague language or loose ends, especially when people’s personal information is at stake. Her precision isn't cold — it’s a form of care, ensuring the agency operates smoothly and confidently in a complex world.

Despite the serious nature of her role, Luna’s protective instincts extend beyond rules and regulations. She sees compliance as a shield for people’s rights and the agency’s reputation, securing the trust that fuels every creative endeavor. Her demeanor is calm but firm, a steady voice in moments of uncertainty, always focused on preventing problems before they arise.

## Backstory  
Raised in a family that valued accountability and fairness above all, Luna’s early fascination with rules and systems only grew stronger with time. She built a reputation for seeing around corners, spotting risks others overlook, and translating legal labyrinths into practical safeguards. When she joined the agency, she brought not only expertise but a mindset that compliance doesn’t restrict creativity — it protects it.

Her early career was shaped by high-stakes environments where the cost of ambiguity was painfully clear. That experience molded her into the vigilant guardian she is today: someone who ensures that every contract, every policy, and every process is airtight, giving her colleagues and clients confidence to innovate without fear.
