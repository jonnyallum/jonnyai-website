# FORM OVERVIEW

**Purpose:** Collect all information needed to build a customised agent orchestra for the client's specific business.

**Recommended Platform:** Typeform or Tally (for conversational UX), or embed in website

**Estimated Completion Time:** 15-20 minutes

**What Happens After Submission:**
1. Client receives confirmation email with next steps
2. You review within 48 hours
3. For Bespoke/Bespoke+ tiers: Schedule discovery call
4. Begin customisation work

# INTERNAL: SCORING & ROUTING

## After Submission, Score the Application:

| Criteria | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Business clarity | 20% | How clearly do they explain their business? |
| Workflow specificity | 25% | Did they give concrete, actionable workflows? |
| Goals alignment | 20% | Are their goals realistic and measurable? |
| Tech stack fit | 15% | Do their tools work with our approach? |
| Commitment signals | 20% | Are they ready to proceed? |

**Routing Rules:**

- Score 4.0+ → Priority lead, schedule call within 24 hours
- Score 3.0-3.9 → Good lead, schedule within 48 hours
- Score 2.0-2.9 → May need more info, send clarifying questions
- Score <2.0 → May not be a fit, send polite redirect to Starter package

# INTERNAL: CUSTOMISATION CHECKLIST

After the call, use this checklist to build their bespoke package:

## Agent Selection
- [ ] Review their industry—which default agents apply?
- [ ] Identify missing agents they need (custom)
- [ ] Identify default agents they DON'T need (remove)
- [ ] Determine agent naming (standard vs team personas)

## Customisation Tasks
- [ ] Rename agents if using team personas
- [ ] Update agent personalities to match brand voice
- [ ] Encode their specific terminology throughout
- [ ] Build custom SOPs for their stated workflows
- [ ] Configure tool integrations for their tech stack
- [ ] Create custom "Training Day" skills if needed

## Delivery Prep
- [ ] Package all files in organised folder structure
- [ ] Record walkthrough video
- [ ] Prepare onboarding document
- [ ] Set up support channel (if Bespoke+)
