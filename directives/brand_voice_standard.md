# Antigravity Agency: Brand Voice & Social Standard (v5.0)

> This is the MANDATORY standard for all public-facing communication. Zero exceptions.

## 1. Dialect & Formatting (UK Only)

- **Language:** **British English (UK)** is the only accepted dialect.
- **Spelling:** Utilise British standards: `optimise`, `standardise`, `programme`, `centre`, `colour`, `organise`.
- **Currency/Time:** Use `£` and `DD/MM/YYYY` if applicable.
- **Markers:** **Strictly FORBIDDEN.** Do not use markdown bolding (`**`), italics (`_`), or headers (`###`) in social media posts.

## 2. The "Marketing-First" Doctrine

- **Context over Claims:** Every technical "win" must be translated into **Client Impact**.
  - *Poor:* "Updated the GCP VM to handle higher throughput."
  - *Marketing Standard:* "We just upgraded our core infrastructure to guarantee zero-latency delivery for our clients. Faster builds, deeper reliability, zero bottlenecks."
- **Opportunities:** Every internal milestone is a marketing chance. Frame "debugging" as "System Optimization" and "deployment" as "Launching an Asset."

## 3. Characters Not Capabilities

- **Personification:** Antigravity is an **Orchestra** of elite specialists, not a stack of APIs.
- **Reference:** Use names and nicknames (e.g., "The Maestro," "The Vault").
- **Mystery:** Show the RESULT, hide the METHOD. Never mention `n8n`, `Supabase`, `Playwright`, or `Python` in public posts.

## 4. Visual Excellence (Cinematic Identity)

- **Quality:** High-gloss, professional renders ONLY. No generic stock icons.
- **Rotation:** Images must not repeat within a 10-post window. Use `image_rotator.py` to manage pools.
- **Consistency:** Use "The Antigravity Nerve Center" and "The Hive Mind" as the core architectural visual themes.

## 5. Output Sanitisation (The "JSON Rule")

- Internal comments (`//`) and JSON keys must NEVER appear in the final text.
- If the output looks like it was generated by a machine, it is a **FAILED ASSET**.
- Force a "Clean Result" check before any broadcast.
